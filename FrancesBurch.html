Overview

I was born in NYC and spent my early childhood in Port Washington, but grew up in Nashville, TN. I received my Bachelor of Arts from Wells College in Aurora, NY, in 1993. The following year, I moved to NYC. Shortly afterward, I met my husband. We have lived in downtown Manhattan ever since.

Professional Background and Education

I have worked primarily as a web developer in agency environments in the NYC metro area. My industry experience includes real estate, music publishing, non-profit, finance, and health care. I completed the first training program in Microsoft .NET technology offered at Baruch College CAPS in the early '00s, along with a 3 month immersive at SetFocus in Parsippany, NJ immediately following.
